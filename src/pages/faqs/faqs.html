What is a Hazard

A hazard is anything in the workplace that has the potential to harm people.Hazards can include objects in the workplace, such as machinery or dangerous chemicals. Other hazards relate to the way work is done.

What is an Incident

An Incident at work is a "discrete occurrence in the course of work" leading to physical or mental occupational injury.
